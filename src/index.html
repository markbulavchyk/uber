8 reasons to start working with us

No cash, payment is automatic
The cost of the trip is paid by the passenger using a linked bank card, i.e. there are no risks associated with the transportation of cash for either the passenger or the driver, you do not need to look for small change.
Commission 8% + BONUSES
We have a minimum commission and we pay bonuses for each trip, some of our competitors keep the bonuses for themselves. We work honestly and with us drivers earn more!
High income with guaranteed payments
We pay wages to the driver's personal bank card! You do not need to register an LLC, individual entrepreneur, pay taxes, file a declaration, pay for accounting services and bank account maintenance.
Work at a convenient time, no schedules
You simply press the button to go online to start working, when you decide to finish, press to go offline. You can work only in the morning, evening, on weekends or holidays!
Comfortable, transparent and simple connection
We connect remotely and free of charge, you do not need to come to us! You send us copies of documents, we register you and send data for connection, in 1-2 days you can start working!
Always a large number of orders
The application shows where there are currently the most orders and where increased rates apply. You will not have downtime and empty runs. Our passengers are always pleasant and absolutely non-conflict people, who are pleasant to drive.
24/7 support
Uber has 24/7 support for drivers via email. If something is unclear, you can always ask a question via On-line consultation.
Without checkers and stickers
You don’t need to change the color, install “checkers” on the car and cover it with stickers.
